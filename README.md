#Potato Disease Detection !!
A dedicated project focused on identifying and diagnosing diseases in potato plants using advanced technologies and machine learning techniques. Potatoes are one of the world's most important food crops, and early disease detection plays a vital role in maintaining crop health, improving yields, and ensuring food security. This project leverages innovative image-processing and artificial intelligence methods to accurately detect and classify potato diseases, enabling farmers and agricultural professionals to take timely preventive and corrective actions. By supporting smarter crop management, the project aims to reduce losses, enhance productivity, and promote sustainable agricultural practices.
